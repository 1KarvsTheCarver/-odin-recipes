Strawberry Sauce

Description

This simple tasty strawberry sauce requires no cooking and is great for summer!

Ingredients

  • 2 quarts fresh strawberries, cleaned, hulled and sliced
  • Â½ cup white sugar

Steps

  1. Place the strawberries in a large bowl. Sprinkle the sugar evenly over the berries, and stir to evenly coat all of the fruit.

  2. Let stand at room temperature for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally. Cover and chill until ready to serve.
