Habanero Syrup

Description

Simple syrup infused with habanero peppers is a spicy and colorful addition to tequila-based cocktails or even lemonade

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 habanero peppers

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil in a saucepan. Slowly stir sugar into boiling water until sugar is dissolved, 2 to 3 minutes. Add habanero peppers to sugar-water mixture, reduce heat to medium-low, and simmer until syrup is thickened and pepper has slightly colored the syrup, about 15 minutes.

  2. Remove saucepan from heat and let syrup cool, about 1 hour. Remove peppers from syrup and store syrup in a glass jar.
